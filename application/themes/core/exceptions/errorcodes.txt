  Clansuite Errorcodes
  --------------------

  You can! use PHP's trigger_error() method to trigger one of the follwing errors.
  But this is not recommended! Our advise is to use Exceptions instead.

  E_USER_WARNING
  E_USER_NOTICE
  E_USER_ERROR
